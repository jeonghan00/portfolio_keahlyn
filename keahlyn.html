About Me
Good Day!
Hi, I’m Keahlyn N. Eudela. I graduated from senior high school at Arellano University Jose Rizal Campus under the General Academic Strand, also known as GAS. I had the opportunity to be included three times in the With Honors list. In addition to that, I also participated in our section’s poster-making activity. To represent our section, we joined our batch’s dance contest and won 1st place for our performance.
Gallery
January 10, 2024 — this was the day the six of us friends were finally together again. It was our first picture as a complete group back when we were in senior high school, since we were in different strands and only had limited chances to spend time together — unlike back in Grade 8, when we used to bond more often.
This is the only picture I have with my grandmother and grandfather, because I never had the chance to meet them in person. I always wished I could see them, but sadly, they are both in heaven now.
‘SAY THE NAME, SEVENTEEN!’ — This group is the one that always brings me joy whenever I feel sad. They are my comfort, especially through their music. Even though their songs are in a different language, I can truly feel the emotions and messages they convey. Their self-written songs deeply connect with the listeners. I always watch ‘Going Seventeen,’ and I’m always excited to see the release of their new music
I really like arts, that’s why I’ve been learning how to draw. But lately, I haven’t had much time to draw because I’ve been busy with schoolwork. Still, I manage to draw whenever I have free time. It’s my hobby, especially when I get bored at home — I often sketch. I only use charcoal since that’s what I’m comfortable with; when it comes to using color, I’m not really good at it.
